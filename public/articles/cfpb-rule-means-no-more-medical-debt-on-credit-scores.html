For millions of Americans struggling with medical debt, relief is on the horizon. Starting March 2025, medical bills will no longer cast a shadow over your credit score

Why This Matters

  • ‍$49 Billion Gone: About 15 million Americans will see medical debt disappear from credit reports.‍
  • More Opportunities: This change could lead to 22,000 more mortgage approvals every year.‍
  • Better Lending Decisions: Medical debt isn’t a good predictor of whether someone will repay loans, so it’s out!

Key Details

This doesn’t mean your medical bills vanish. You’re still responsible for paying them—just without the extra pressure on your credit.

What Can You Do?

  • Check your credit report regularly for updates
  • Dispute any inaccurate bills with tools like DebtCat
  • Negotiate payment plans directly with providers

This CFPB rule is a big step toward fairer lending and fewer sleepless nights over medical debt. Stay ahead by understanding your rights and using resources to manage your debt.
