Refund Policy

Effective Date: October 3, 2024

We're Sorry, But We Do Not Provide Refunds

At DebtCat, we strive to offer exceptional services to our customers. Due to the immediate processing and fulfillment of our services, we are unable to offer refunds once a transaction has been completed.

1. All Sales are Final

We are committed to delivering high-quality services promptly. All transactions are considered final upon completion of the purchase.

2. Thin Margins and Transactional Nature

Our business operates on thin margins, and we initiate service delivery immediately after an order is placed. This includes incurring costs from third-party providers and initiating processes that cannot be halted or reversed. As a result, we cannot offer refunds in most cases.
