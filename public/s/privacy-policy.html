We automatically collect certain information when you visit, use, or navigate the Services. This may include:

We use cookies, web beacons, pixels (including the Metapixel provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. on certain pages), and other tracking technologies to collect information about your interactions with our Services. We use your personal information for a variety of business purposes, including:

  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from public authorities.

We process your personal information based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill our contractual obligations to you.
